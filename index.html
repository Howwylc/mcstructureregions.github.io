Structure Region Viewer

This is a model to help visualize structure regions in Minecraft. I recommend zooming your browser out to get a full view of the regions.

  • The green sections represent areas in which the structure can spawn.
  • The red sections represent areas in which the structure cannot spawn.
  • These regions repeat infinitely in the same pattern of squares. Regions 1400 blocks from (0,0) are modeled.
